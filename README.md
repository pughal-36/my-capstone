# Decision Journal

A small app for logging decisions before you make them — your reasoning, 
your confidence level, and what you expect to happen — so you can look 
back later and see how your judgment actually performs over time.

## Why
Most decisions get made and forgotten. This app makes you write down 
your reasoning *before* you know the outcome, then lets you revisit and 
grade yourself later. It's a lightweight tool for building better 
judgment through reflection.
